Minttukaakao

Description

Who doesn't love hot chocolate. The only thing better than hot chocolate would be hot chocolate with a pinch of vodka. Imagine yourself under the Northern Lights in a cold winter night drinking hot chocolate to warm up your soul.

Finnish adult version of hot chocolate adds a little bit of mint vodka to flavour it up and warm you up. The local variant of mint vodka traditionally used is Minttuviina. Minttuviina is a brand name vodka.

The name of the drink in Finnish is Minttukaakao, which means mint hot chocolate. You can make Minttukaakao without alcohol also for everybody to enjoy.

Ingredients

Measurements for 2 portions/glasses

  • 5 dl milk
  • 3 tablespoons dark chocolate powder
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • Â½ dl mint vodka
  • 2 mint chocolate pieces
  • 1 dl whipped cream

Steps

  1. Heat up milk.
  2. Whisk in chocolate powder and sugar.
  3. Mix in peppermint vodka.
  4. Whisk the whipped cream to foam.
  5. Place the mint chocolate pieces inside the drinking glasses.
  6. Pour the hot drink on top of the chocolate pieces.
  7. Add the whipped cream and any additional flavouring on top of the drink.
